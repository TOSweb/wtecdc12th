Conference Program:

Dive into a three-day agenda crafted to empower the Tamil economic community:

  • Keynote Addresses: Hear Tamil and global leaders share insights on economic trends
  • Plenary Sessions: Discuss strategies for Tamil businesses in global markets.
  • B2B Matchmaking: Connect with Tamil and international firms for partnerships.
  • Technology Showcases: Explore Tamil-led innovations in a 3-day exhibition (booth size: 10ft x 10ft, 2 persons per booth).
  • Success Stories: Learn from Tamil entrepreneurs’ journeys to success.
  • Networking Events: Build relationships with Tamil influencers and peers.
  • Awards Ceremony: Honor Tamil business excellence with the Crown Jewel of Business Leaders Awards.
  • Cultural Programs: Celebrate Tamil heritage with vibrant performances.

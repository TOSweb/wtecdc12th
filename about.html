About Us

About the Conference

Since 2009, the World Tamils Economic Conference has been a catalyst for economic empowerment within the global Tamil diaspora. Hosted in cities like Chennai, Dubai, and Kuala Lumpur, it has sparked transformative partnerships and initiatives that uplift Tamil communities worldwide. The 12th edition, held in Washington, D.C., USA. amplifies this legacy by uniting Tamil entrepreneurs, policymakers, and influencers to shape a prosperous future.

This summit fosters collaboration across borders, showcases Tamil-led innovations, and addresses global economic challenges. From startups to multinational corporations, participants will find a platform to exchange ideas, forge alliances, and drive sustainable growth

Objectives
  • Empower Tamil entrepreneurs with global business opportunities
  • Facilitate partnerships between Tamil businesses and international markets
  • Highlight Tamil contributions to technology and innovation
  • Promote policies that support Tamil economic development
  • Celebrate the cultural and economic vibrancy of the Tamil diaspora
